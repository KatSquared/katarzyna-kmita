final project on cognitive biases

Video mockup of an interactive educational website utilising interactive visual communication as an effective learning tool - final project in Graphic Design.

The ThoughtLab's main objective is to educate on specific topics in cognitive psychology and related philosophical issues. These topics include intellectual virtues such as imagination, intellectual modesty, curiosity and many more.

The website targets individuals of any age group, except for children, and aims to reach the widest possible audience, irrespective of their background, culture, or personal differences. It is tailored to individuals who possess the ability for self-criticism and have a desire for self-education and self-improvement.

ThoughtLab's content aims to communicate with viewers primarily through interactive visual means, appealing to their subconscious mind. The textual elements serve as supplementary means for engaging the conscious level of cognition.
